一个普通的周末，Clio 加入 MindsEvo 创意工作坊，成为第一位年轻创作者。两天内，我们共同完成了三个原创创意设计和四个交互式网页游戏。整个过程中，Clio 积极参与了头脑风暴、设计、实施讨论、测试、验证和改进迭代。这两天成为了创意工作坊概念的第一个完整案例。 During an ordinary weekend, Clio joined MindsEvo Creative Workshop as its first young creator. Together, we completed three original creative designs and four interactive web games. Throughout the process, Clio actively participated in brainstorming, design, implementation discussions, testing, verification, and improvement. These two days became the first complete example of the Creative Workshop concept.

难忘时刻Memorable Moments

三个让人记住的瞬间Three Moments Worth Remembering

"这是第几次'快好了'？""Which 'soon' is this?"

等爷爷做饭时，Clio 一直追问什么时候可以开始开发，还开玩笑地数了爷爷说了几次"快了"。While waiting for Grandpa to finish cooking, Clio kept asking when development would begin and jokingly counted how many times Grandpa had said "soon."

是时候回去工作了Time to Go Back to Work

在操场玩耍休息一段时间后，Clio 主动提议：After taking a break at the playground, Clio suggested:

"我们去工作吧。""We should go back to work."

这不是被要求的，而是出于真正的热情。This reflected genuine enthusiasm rather than being asked to continue.

理解 MindsEvoUnderstanding MindsEvo

聊完 logo 和公司名字的含义后，Clio 开始用平台理念来评价新创意。其中一个例子是：她意识到"兔子帽"最初的形式主要是娱乐性的，而不是思维训练。After discussing the logo and the meaning of the company name, Clio began applying the platform philosophy when evaluating new ideas. One example was recognizing that the Rabbit Hat idea, in its original form, was mainly for entertainment rather than thinking training.

导师手记Mentor's Notes

每个创作者都有自己独特的思维方式。我们的目标不是评判一个想法的好坏，而是帮助每位创作者发现、发展，并逐步将自己的想法变为现实。Every creator has a unique way of thinking. Our goal is not to judge whether an idea is good or bad, but to help each creator discover, develop, and gradually turn their own ideas into reality.

这两天里，Clio 不仅创作了几个有趣的游戏，也开始理解创意工作坊的精神——共同思考、讨论、改进和创造。During these two days, Clio not only created several interesting games but also began to understand the spirit of Creative Workshop — thinking, discussing, improving, and creating together.

这才是她创作旅程真正的开始。This is the true beginning of her creative journey.
