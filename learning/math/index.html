Learning Foundation · Math Thinking

数学启智 Math Thinking

我们不覆盖数学教材，我们从数学情境中抽取元思维操作。无论什么知识点，只要孩子在真正执行「比较、建模、推断、发现规律」，训练就在发生。 We don't cover the math curriculum — we extract meta-thinking operations from math contexts. As long as children are genuinely executing comparison, modeling, inference, or pattern recognition, the training is happening.

难度分级参考年级知识背景，仅供设计定位，不是孩子的课程表。 Difficulty levels reference grade knowledge backgrounds for design purposes only — not a curriculum sequence for children.

RootGene: Comparison

比较Comparison

判断大小、多少、长短。每次操作都是一次明确的比较元思维练习，从 10 以内到 100 以内递进。Judge bigger, smaller, more, fewer. Each round is an explicit comparison meta-thinking exercise, progressing from within-10 to within-100.

RootGene: Comparison, Multi-dim

数感Number Sense

拆分、接近、凑数——三种题型交替出现，训练从多个角度看待同一个数的能力。Split, proximity, complete — three alternating question types that train seeing one number from multiple angles.
